The Challenge

The compliance burden is real.
And it's been accepted for too long.

Most financial institutions are managing Licensed Representative compliance the same way they did a decade ago — on spreadsheets, in email threads, across disconnected vendor portals. The cost is high. The risk is real. It doesn't have to be this way.

💸
High Cost of Compliance

Your compliance team's time is expensive. Every hour spent manually filling Form 3s, chasing background check results, or reconciling CPD records is an hour not spent on work that moves the business forward. The cost compounds quietly — and the mistakes add regulatory risk on top.

🔗
No Centralised Vendor Hub

Background screening goes to one portal. L&D to another. CMFAS tracking somewhere else. AML/CFT checks to yet another system. No unified view. No automated SLA monitoring. No way to know where things stand without picking up the phone.

⚙️
Lack of Automated Processes

Form 3 workflows. CPD tracking. Recertification cycles. Fit & Proper renewals. Each one a manual process, each one a source of delays, errors, and audit exposure. The representative lifecycle never stops — but the automation still hasn't started.

🤖
No AI-Driven Innovation

When MAS issues a new notice, does your team find out immediately — or days later? Can your system tell you which representatives have CMFAS gaps before MAS asks? AI can surface these answers in seconds. Most firms are still doing it by hand.

Regnify is built for MAS-licensed financial institutions that employ or supervise Licensed Representatives — fund management companies, securities firms, insurance companies, and financial advisory firms. If your firm carries regulatory responsibility for representatives under the Financial Advisers Act or Securities and Futures Act, Regnify was built for you.
Everything, from day one to departure. Pre-onboarding covers Form 3A/B/C/D digital workflows, Fit & Proper checks, CMFAS mapping, background screening, and RNF submission. Post-onboarding handles CPD tracking, recertification, breach logging, and activity attestation. Cessation manages the exit process and MAS notifications cleanly. And the Rep Passport gives each representative a portable, auditable career record they carry with them — useful for future employers too.
Regnify is an ecosystem, not just another tool. Background screening partners, L&D providers, CMFAS examination partners, and AML/CFT/PF vendors are all integrated directly into the platform. Your team works through one interface, with automated SLA tracking and report cross-referencing built in. No more vendor portals, no more manual follow-ups, no more piecing results together in a spreadsheet.
